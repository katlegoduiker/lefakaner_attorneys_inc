About Us

 

Where Client Satisfaction Takes Precedence

Lefakane Attorneys Inc is a black female-owned boutique law firm providing niche legal services for you and your business.

Our Managing Director, Ms.Refilwe Lefakane, has an LLB from the University of the Witwatersrand and is an Admitted Attorney of the High Court with over 10 years’ legal experience.

Ms. Lefakane has expertise in Corporate Governance, Commercial Litigation, Contractual Drafting, Labour Law, Family Law, Procurement Law, Intellectual Property Law, Broadcasting Law, Competition Law and Third-Party Claims.
